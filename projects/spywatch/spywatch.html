Project

Spy Watch

Back to projects

Overview

Wearable compact watch device that can read temperature, humnidity, lumens, take photos, and transmit this data to a backend website.

Constraints

  • Limited physical space for electronics and wiring inside a wearable form factor.
  • Need for stable power delivery to embedded components.
  • Mechanical packaging constraints while maintaining accessibility and assembly.
  • Balancing functionality with a compact and wearable design.

Electrical Design

Electrical Design

  • Integrated embedded electronics into a compact wearable system architecture.
  • Designed regulated power delivery to support reliable operation of onboard components.
  • Managed component placement and wiring in a constrained layout.
  • Considered signal flow between sensing, processing, and output functions.
  • Built around system reliability and practical hardware integration.

Challenges / Iterations

  • Iterated on packaging and component placement to fit the design within the watch form factor.
  • Worked through power delivery and integration issues associated with compact embedded hardware.
  • Refined the balance between mechanical design constraints and electrical functionality.
  • Improved system organization through repeated prototyping and testing.

Next Steps

  • Further miniaturize the design for a more refined wearable package.
  • Improve system robustness and long-term power management.
  • Expand software and backend functionality for better real-time interaction.
  • Refine the mechanical enclosure for better manufacturability and assembly.
